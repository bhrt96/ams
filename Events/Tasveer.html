Rules and Regulation

  • EVENT GUIDELINES:
    • Themes for the event:
      • BLACK & WHITE (B&W)
      • STREET PHOTOGRAPHY
      • SILHOUETTE
    • It is a single round Online Event.
    • Participants should mail their photographs with the theme.
    • The photograph should be accompanied by a suitable Caption.
    • The SUBJECT of your mail when sending entries should strictly be the "Theme Name".
    • A participant is allowed to send Multiple entries per theme.
    • Entries should be mailed to "ams.society@iiita.ac.in" and should include the following in the description:
      • The caption
      • Your College Name
      • Participant Name
    • Entries should reach us before 19:59:59 on 18th Oct, 2015.
  • RULES:
    • This event is open to all colleges.
    • Alumnus from different colleges are also invited to participate in the event.
    • The picture should be saved as " Participant's College_Participant's Name.jpeg ". In case of ALUMNI,
      the picture should be in the form " Participant's College_Participant's Name_Passout Year.jpeg "
    • The entries are to be accompanied with a caption not longer than 20 words.
    • Copyright : The copyright in all images submitted to the Competition will remain with the owner who will
      be credited. Each entrant grants to the Organizers a license to use their images in relation to the
      Competition and the Exhibition, if required.
    • There must not be any Watermarks, Copyright marks or any Text/Graphic on the picture.
    • Please limit your adjustment to minor changes of cropping, sharpening and contrast. Avoid significant
      over or under color saturation. Dodging (to brighten shadows) or burning (to darken highlights) is allowed,
      but the alterations should enable the photograph to closely resemble what you see. Please avoid any
      extensive manipulation.
    • The participant should be ready with the original image when asked upon by the organizers.
    • Images should not exceed 8 MB in size.
    • Stitched panoramas are allowed.
    • The photo should not have been previously entered in any competition/exhibition.
    • PLAGIARISM should be strictly avoided and such photographs shall be immediately disqualified from the competition.
    • The Evaluation Criteria is as follows :
      • 40% - Number of Facebook likes
      • 10% - Caption
      • 50% - Judges' ratings
    • The theme and originality of the photograph will also be considered.
    • Each theme will have its own Individual winner. Overall i.e there will be 3 Separate Winners. In case of
      Alumni, the best photograph in each theme will be appreciated.
    • The decision of the organizers shall be final and binding on all contestants.
    • The photographs submitted by all the participants will be uploaded
      on https://www.facebook.com/effervescence.iiita
    • Photographs will be updated every day once in between 8 PM - 11 PM. Once uploaded, the participant
      will be acknowledged through an e-mail. The final result will be announced on 19th October.
