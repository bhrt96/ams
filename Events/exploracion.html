Rules and Regulation

  1. AIM - To catch glimpses of beauty of the campus of IIIT - Allahabad. To innovate and see the campus in a new way the way you want to see it not the way its meant to be.

  2. EVENT STRUCTURE

    • Its a single round online event strictly for FRESHERS (B2k15) .
    • Participants are required to explore various parts of the campus and capture it.
    • The place time may vary according to their convinience.
    • Its the oppourtunity for participants to explore the beauty of campus and know more about it .
    • Also its the chance for freshers to show their innovation and photography talent.
    • The picture can contain panorama,collage,grid etc. and editing at any level.
    • So its time to show your talent.
  3. RULES

    • Participants are strictly adviced to capture pictures of IIIT - Allahabad campus only.
    • Picture size should not exceed 8MB.
    • Participants are required to send their entries before 23:59:59 on 3rd September 2015.
    • Judging shall be done on the basis of caption and innovation in the picture and number of likes on facebook.
    • Minimum 1 entry from a participant is compulsory.
    • A participant is allowed a Maximum of 3 entries.
    • The picture should be saved as " Participant's Enrollment No_Entry no.jpg ". (Eg:- iec2014025_1.jpg)
    • The entries are to be accompanied with a caption not longer than 20 words.
    • Copyright : The copyright in all images submitted to the Competition will remain with the owner who will be credited. Each entrant grants to the Organizers a license to use their images in relation to the Competition and the Exhibition, if required.
    • The participant should be ready with the original image when asked upon by the organisers
    • STITCHED PANORAMAS,COLLAGES,GRIDS and extensive editing are allowed.
    • Plagiarism should be strictly avoided and such participants shall be immediately disqualified from the competition.
    • If anybody found taking pictures at restricted areas of the campus will be completely responsible for it AMS does not take any responsibility of it.
    • The photographs submitted by participants will be uploaded on THUNDERBOLT AMS
    • Photographs will be uploaded everyday only once from 6-7 pm only (irrespective of the time the entries had been sent).
  4. Evaluation Criteria is as follows :-
    • 40% - facebook likes
    • 10% - caption
    • 50% - judge's rating

  5. Prize Money - Worth Rs. 4000 to 3 worthy victors.
